  -
    type: paragraph
    attrs:
      textAlign: left
    content:
      -
        type: text
        text: 'Traditional design approaches often focused on isolated screens or individual features. Modern experience design, however, views the product as a connected ecosystem where every interaction influences the next. This shift places the user journey at the center of all design decisions. Organizations are realizing that user frustration often comes from poorly connected flows rather than missing functionality. Experience-led design identifies these gaps and redesigns interactions to feel cohesive and intentional. This approach ensures that users are guided smoothly, without confusion or unnecessary effort.'
